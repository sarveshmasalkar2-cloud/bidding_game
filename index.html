      How To Play

      1. One player creates a room and shares the code.
      2. Others join with that code.
      3. Everyone gets their own unique prompt — 60 seconds to draw it.
      4. Drawings go to the Auction Hall — anonymous, one at a time.
      5. Blind bid on each painting. Highest bid wins.
      6. After all lots are sold, secret values are revealed.
        Final score = Remaining Balance + Sum of True Values of Owned Paintings.
